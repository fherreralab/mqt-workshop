2nd Workshop on Molecular Quantum Technology MQT 2022

December 12-16, 2022. Puerto Natales, Chile.

MQT 2022 is an in-person workshop that will bring together scientists across disciplines to discuss fundamental science and applications where molecules and molecular materials could contribute to the field of quantum technology in novel ways, as well as closely related topics.

The first edition of the workshop was held in 2019 (http://mqt2019.org), attracting a very engaging group of renowned scientists and early career researchers across physics, chemistry and materials science, with a common interest in this emerging area of interdisciplinary quantum science.

Workshop Venue

  • MQT2022 will be held at the Hotel Costaustralis, located in the beautiful city of Puerto Natales, in the Chilean Patagonia. The hotel is only a short trip from Torres del Paine National Park
